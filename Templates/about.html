About Me

Following a successful career in graphic design I started my own business and began my journey as an event photographer 15 years ago. Since then I have expanded into wedding photography and portrait work. As a photographer my role is to be an unseen presence at your event, capturing the beautiful natural moments and memorializing your event in a personal and professional way.


I offer multiple fixed packages and also a bespoke package, so that we can meet your every need. For larger events I can recommend a second photographer to ensure that we capture the essence of the whole event.

Wedding

As part of your big day my focus is to capture those special moments so that you can live them. My aim is to preserve those moments for you to enjoy in the years to come and enable you to be transported back to the happiest day of your life.

Party

For those special moments in life like a 50th Wedding Anniversary to a sweet 16th birthday party I can be there to preserve every minute of it. I can interact with your guests to make unique moments or be in the background for those more natural shots.

Events

Whether it be a graduation, gig or a corporate event I can be there to immortalize those big moments. It can be a photo booth or on the fly where I will take the photos that really make you remember that moment.
